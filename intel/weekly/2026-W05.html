1

Data Collection

Raw data is enriched with MITRE ATT&CK mappings, ransomware campaign linkages (from CISA advisories and vendor reports), and geographic attribution of infrastructure.

Structured Analysis

We apply Intelligence Community-standard Structured Analytical Techniques (SATs) including Key Assumptions Check, Analysis of Competing Hypotheses, and Indicators of Change to reduce cognitive bias.

4

Confidence Assessment

All judgments include confidence levels (High/Medium/Low) based on source reliability, information credibility, and corroboration across multiple sources, following ICD 203 standards.

5

Plain Language Translation

Technical findings are translated into business-relevant language to support executive decision-making. We explain not just "what" but "why it matters."
