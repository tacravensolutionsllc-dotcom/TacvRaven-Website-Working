Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our study tools

Is it really 100% free?
Yes, completely free - no credit card, no "freemium" upsells, no premium tier. TalonPrep and NetForge+ are lead magnets for our professional tools (BlackFeather & Corvus Engine). We want you to pass your cert and remember us when you're in a SOC someday.
Are questions updated for current exams?
Yes. TalonPrep covers the current Security+ SY0-701 objectives. NetForge+ covers the current Network+ N10-009 objectives. We update questions as CompTIA updates their exam objectives.
Can I track my progress?
Yes. Both tools track your performance by domain, show your accuracy trends, and highlight weak areas. Progress is saved locally in your browser - no account needed. Clear your browser data and it resets.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Both tools are fully responsive and work on phones, tablets, and desktops. Perfect for studying on the go.
How many questions are there?
TalonPrep has 400+ Security+ questions across all 6 domains. NetForge+ has 820+ Network+ questions plus interactive PBQ labs. We're continuously adding more based on user feedback and exam updates.
Will these guarantee I pass?
No practice tool can guarantee a pass - anyone who claims otherwise is lying. What we can say: our questions mirror real exam difficulty, our explanations teach underlying concepts, and consistent practice with analytics-driven focus on weak areas dramatically improves your odds.
